Humble Beginnings

It was a stormy saturday night in a small country in Southeast Asian and a mother was going to the hospital. She was pregnant and that day had been rough considering she was going into labor. Not only was the storm thonderous but a flood was starting to come on. Racing from her little village in the hills, she and her husband managed to get to the hospital Mother's Hospital (translated). She gave birth to a baby boy and named her the center of the universe.
